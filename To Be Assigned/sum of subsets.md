###### Content:
Suppose $U_1,...,U_m$ are [[subset]]s of $V$. The sum of $U_1,...,U_m$, denoted $U_1+...+U_m$, is the [[set]] of all possible sums of elements of $U_1,...,U_m$. More precisely, $$U_1+...+U_m = \{u_1+...+u_m : u_1 \in U_1,...,u_m \in U_m\}$$
