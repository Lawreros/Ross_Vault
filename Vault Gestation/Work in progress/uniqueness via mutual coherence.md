###### Content:
If there exist $x$ so that $Ax = b$ and $||x||_0 < \frac{1}{2} (1+\frac{1}{\mu (A)})$, then $x$ is the [[sparse]]st solution of problem $(P_0)$: $\underset{x}{min} ||x||_0$ such that $Ax = b$
