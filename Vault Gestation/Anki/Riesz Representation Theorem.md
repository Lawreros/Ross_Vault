###### Content:
Suppose $V$ is a finite-dimensional [[inner product space]] and $\varphi$ is a [[linear functional]] on $V$. Then there is a unique vector $u \in V$ such that $$\varphi(V) = \langle v, u \rangle$$
for every $v \in V$
