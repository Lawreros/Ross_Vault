###### Content:
Suppose $T \in \mathcal{L}(V)$ and $U$ is a [[subspace]] of $V$ [[invariant]] under $T$. The quotient operator $T/U \in \mathcal{L}(V/U)$ is defined by $$(T/U)(v+U)=Tv+U$$ for $v \in V$
