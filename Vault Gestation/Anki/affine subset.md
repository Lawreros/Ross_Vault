###### Content:
- An affine subset of $V$ is a subset of $V$ of the form $v+U$ for some $v \in V$ and some subspace $U$ of $V$.
- For $v \in V$ and $U$ a subspace of $V$, the affine subset $v+U$ is said to be [[parallel]] to $U$.
