###### Content:
Suppose $U_1,...,U_m$ are [[subspace]]s of $V$. The sum of $U_1,...,U_m$, denoted $U_1+...+U_m$, is the smallest [[subspace]] of $V$ containing $U_1,...,U_m$.

In other words: $$U_1+U_2 = span\{U_1 \cup U_2\} = \{x+y:x \in U_1, y \in U_2\}$$
