###### Content:
Each $A \in M_n$ can be written uniquely as $A = H+iK$, in which both $H$ and $K$ are [[Hermitian]]. It can also be written uniquely as $A = H+S$, in which $H$ is Hermitian and $S$ is [[skew Hermitian]].
