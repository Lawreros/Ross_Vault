###### Content:
Suppose $T \in \mathcal{L}(V)$ and $U$ is a [[subspace]] of $V$ [[invariant]] under $T$. The restriction operator $T|_U \in \mathcal{L}(U)$ is defined by $$T|_U(u)=Tu$$ for $u \in U$
