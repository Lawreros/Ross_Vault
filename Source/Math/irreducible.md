###### Properties:
- Let $A \in M_n$. $A$ is irreducible if and only if the [[entry directed graph]] $\Gamma(A)$ is [[strongly connected]]
- If $A \in M_n$ is a [[nonnegative matrix]] and irreducible, then the [[spectral radius]] $\rho(A) > 0$
