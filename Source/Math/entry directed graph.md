###### Content:
Let $A\in M_n$. We associate with $A$ the entry digraph $\Gamma (A)$ whose vertex set is $\{1,2,...,n\}$ and $\forall i,j \in \{1,2,...,n\}$, the edge $(i,j) \in \Gamma(A)$ if and only if $a_{ij}\neq 0$.
