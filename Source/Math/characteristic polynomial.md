###### Content:
Thought of as a formal polynomial in t, the characteristic polynomial of $A \in M_n$ is $$p_A(t) = det(tI-A)$$ We refer to the equation $p_A(t)=0$ as the characteristic equation of $A$.

- [[Cayley-Hamilton Theorem]]
