###### Content:
Let $V, ||\cdot||$ over [[field]] $\mathcal{K}$ be a [[normed linear space]]. If $x \in V$, then there exists some linear function $f \in V'$ such that $||f||_{V'} =1$ and $f(x) = ||x||$
