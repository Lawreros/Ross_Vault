###### Content:
The spectrum of $A \in M_n$ is the set of all $\lambda \in \textbf{C}$ that are [[eigenvalue]]s of $A$; we denote this set by $\sigma(A)$.

###### Properties:
- The spectrums of two [[similar]] matrices are the same.
