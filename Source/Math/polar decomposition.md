###### Content:
For all $A \in M_n$ there exists some [[positive semidefinite]] matrix $P \in M_n$ and [[unitary matrix]] $W \in M_n$ such that $A=PW$

**Proof:**
Say $A = U\Sigma V^*$ is an [[SVD]] of $A$. Then $A = U\Sigma U^* UV^*$ where $P = U\Sigma U^*$ and $W = UV^*$
