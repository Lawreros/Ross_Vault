###### Content:
Let $||\cdot||$ be a [[matrix norm]] on $M_n$. For any $A \in M_n$, the condition number of $A$ is $\mathcal{K}(A) := ||A||||A^{-1}||$

*"The condition number is a measure of how stable a matrix is to inversion (larger = bad, 1 = best)"*
