###### Content:
A [[polynomial]] $s \in \mathcal{P}(\textbf{F})$ is called a factor of $p \in \mathcal{P}(\textbf{F})$ if there exists a polynomial $q \in \mathcal{P}(\textbf{F})$ such that $p = sq$.
