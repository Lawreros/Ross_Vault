###### Content:
The complement of any event $A$ is the event "not $A$", i.e. the event that $A$ does not occur. The complement of an event $A$ is usually denoted as $A^C$.

###### Properties:
- $P(A^C) = 1-P(A)$
- $P(A) = P(AB)+P(AB^C)$
