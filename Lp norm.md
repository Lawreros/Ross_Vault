###### Content:
For a real number $p \geq 1$, the p-norm or Lp norm of a vector $x$ is defined by $$||x||_p = (|x_1|^p + |x_2|^p+...+|x_n|^p)^{1/p}$$

###### Properties:
- The Euclidean norm falls into this class and is the 2-norm
